DashBird captures everything that happens inside and out of your car. Its 280 degrees of vision captures would-be vandals and protects you on the road by retelling your side of the story in case of an accident. The movement of your vehicle automatically activates DashBird and its onboard memory holds 24 hours worth of video. The DashBird app lets you share funny or scary moments on the road with on Facebook, Instagram, or Vine instantly.

DashBird's internal sensors detect car accidents and can send for help by alerting friends or family of your location. DashBird costs less than a LoJack device yet lets you access it from anywhere in the world via your smartphone. In the event your car is stolen you can choose to share recent GPS information with law enforcement. DashBird also uses its onboard GPS to analyze your most traveled routes and improve your driving efficiency. And in states where radar detectors are legal DashBird can be set to automatically alert you to the presence of possible radar guns.

FAQ

Does DashBird constantly upload video?
No, although DashBird always connects to the internet via 3G by default it only uploads video to our servers when you tell it to.
Can a thief easily erase my DashBird?
No, DashBird is built with security in mind and will not allow access to your DashBird by unknown parties.
